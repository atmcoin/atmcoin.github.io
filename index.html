PROVIDING EASY ACCESS TO CASH WITH GLOBAL PARTNERS
Just Cash brings together two technologies that are already proven and trusted: ATMs and mobile apps. Access cash in 3 easy steps.
1. Sign In
Sign in to Your Existing Mobile App
No new accounts to create, login details to remember, or balances to transfer.
2. Get Code
Create a Just Cash Code
Select the ATM you would like to withdraw from, select the amount of cash to withdraw, and approve any transaction fees.
3. Your Cash
Enter Just Cash Code, Withdraw Cash
Select Mobile Cash on the ATM home screen, enter the Just Cash Code, and receive cash in seconds!
